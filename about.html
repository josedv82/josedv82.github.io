Jose Fernandez

VP, Performance & R&D at BCSG | Born in Spain, I hold a Bachelor's Degree in Sport Science (with Honors) and a Postgraduate Diploma (PGDip) in Strength and Conditioning from Madrid, complemented by a Master's in High Performance Sport from Australia. I started my career coaching in youth basketball academies, developing talent amid resource constraints—a foundation that shaped my pragmatic approach to performance.

I started my journey in professional sports in 2008, first in European football and then in China's professional basketball league. These early experiences expanded my understanding of diverse sports ecosystems and paved the way for my next role in 2016 with the Houston Astros, in MLB.

Over six seasons at the Astros, I held the role of performance analyst first, before moving on to serve as Head of Sports Science, where I focused on player development and coaching support through athlete monitoring and multiple learning initiatives. Today, I lead the Performance and R&D departments at BCSG, a global sports firm managing soccer teams across multiple international leagues.

With a variety of experiences across multiple sports and environments, I enjoy leveraging data analysis and programming, applied research and player development initiatives to design solutions for elite performance. I am passionate about building systems from the ground up, channeling an entrepreneurial spirit into ventures that merge science, technology and sport.
