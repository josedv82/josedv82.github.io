Jose Fernandez

VP, Performance & R&D at BCSG | Born in Spain and formally trained in Sport Science, I began my career in youth basketball academies, cultivating talent amid resource constraints. My journey expanded across Europe and China, mastering adaptability in diverse sports ecosystems. After six years optimizing athlete performance with MLB’s Houston Astros, I now lead performance strategy and R&D at BCSG, a global sports firm managing soccer teams across top international leagues.
Bridging basketball, baseball, and soccer, I’ve honed expertise in data analysis and programming to design cutting-edge solutions for elite performance. Passionate about building systems from the ground up, I channel an entrepreneurial spirit into ventures that merge science, innovation, and sport.
